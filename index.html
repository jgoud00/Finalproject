⚙ Client-Server Model — How Websites Work

Your browser is the client — it requests pages and displays them. In a real portal, the browser sends your data to a server that queries a database and returns matching scholarships. In this demo everything runs in your browser — data is hard-coded in data.js and logic runs in app.js.

📡 In real applications, scholarship data is fetched from remote APIs over the internet.
